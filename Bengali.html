Welcome to the Bengali Committee!

Bengali dance is a traditional dance form that originates from the Bengal region, which is divided between India and Bangladesh. It is a form of classical dance that has been influenced by various styles such as Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and Manipuri.

About Us

Welcome to the Bengali Dance Committee! We are a group of passionate dancers who share a love for Bengali culture and dance. Our mission is to promote and celebrate Bengali dance in our community by organizing performances, workshops, and cultural events.

Our team consists of talented dancers from different backgrounds who come together to share their knowledge and passion for Bengali dance. We believe that dance is a powerful way to connect with our culture and heritage, and we strive to inspire others to explore the beauty and diversity of Bengali dance.

Whether you are a seasoned dancer or a beginner, we welcome you to join us in our journey to promote and celebrate Bengali dance!
