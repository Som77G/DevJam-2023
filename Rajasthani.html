About Us

We are a group of Rajasthani people who have come together to preserve and promote our rich cultural heritage. Our committee organizes events and activities that celebrate our traditions and provide opportunities for members to connect with each other.

Whether you are a seasoned dancer or a beginner, we welcome you to join us in our journey to promote and celebrate Rajasthani comittee!

Ghoomar or ghumar is a traditional folk dance of Rajasthan. It was the Bhil tribe who performed it to worship Goddess Saraswati which was later embraced by other Rajasthani communities.
