Welcome to the Bhangra Committee!

Bhangra committees are typically groups of individuals who come together to organize and participate in Bhangra performances and competitions. Bhangra is a traditional folk dance from the Punjab region of India, and it has gained popularity all over the world, especially in the Punjabi diaspora.

About Us

Bhangra committees are typically made up of passionate individuals who are dedicated to preserving and promoting Punjabi culture through dance. These committees organize Bhangra performances at weddings, festivals, and other cultural events. They also participate in Bhangra competitions where different groups showcase their skills and creativity. Bhangra committees often spend months preparing for competitions, practicing their dance routines and creating elaborate costumes. They may also work on designing and building their own sets and props to make their performances even more impressive. Through their dedication and hard work, Bhangra committees have helped to popularize Bhangra and promote Punjabi culture around the world. They are an important part of the Bhangra community, and their contributions are highly valued.
